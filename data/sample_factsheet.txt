Company Factsheet: Aether Robotics Inc.

Founded in 2019, Aether Robotics Inc. designs autonomous warehouse robots for
mid-sized logistics companies. The company is headquartered in Austin, Texas,
and employs approximately 140 people across engineering, sales, and support.

Product Line:
Aether's flagship product is the "Pathfinder X3" autonomous mobile robot,
released in 2023. The Pathfinder X3 can carry loads up to 500 kilograms and
navigates warehouses using LiDAR and computer vision, without requiring fixed
floor markers or magnetic strips.

Financial Performance:
For the fiscal year 2025, Aether Robotics reported net revenue of $42 million,
representing a 31% year-over-year increase. Gross margin improved to 38%, up
from 33% in fiscal year 2024. The company attributes this growth to expanded
sales in the e-commerce fulfillment sector.

Leadership:
The company is led by CEO Maria Chen, who previously worked as a robotics
engineer at a major automotive manufacturer. The CTO, David Okafor, joined
in 2021 and oversees the autonomy software team.

Future Plans:
Aether Robotics has announced plans to expand into the European market in
2027, with a new regional office planned for Rotterdam, Netherlands. The
company is also developing a smaller robot model, the "Pathfinder X1",
targeted at small and medium-sized warehouses with tighter aisle spacing.
